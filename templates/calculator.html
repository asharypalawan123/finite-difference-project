Enter your function, evaluation point, step size, and choose a differentiation method. Results show the full step-by-step solution.

Supported Functions

sin(x)
cos(x)

sqrt(x)
log(x)

pi
e

Use ** for exponentiation. Example:  x**2 + sin(x)
